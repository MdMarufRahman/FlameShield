Fire Safety Tips

Access to fire safety tips empowers individuals with the knowledge and skills to respond effectively to fire incidents. Here are some essential fire safety tips:

  • Install smoke alarms on every level of your home, including inside bedrooms and outside sleeping areas.
  • Test smoke alarms every month and replace them if they are more than 10 years old.
  • Create a fire escape plan and practice it with your family at least twice a year.
  • Teach children how to escape on their own in case you can't help them.
  • Avoid overloading electrical outlets and never use damaged electrical cords.
  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources.
  • Never leave cooking food unattended, and keep flammable items away from the stove.
